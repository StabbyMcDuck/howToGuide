Getting Started!

or How I Learned To Love The Yarn

First you will need to make a free Ravelry account.

Head over to https://www.ravelry.com/ and follow the directions.



After signing up for the Ravelry account, you will need to register for a Pro account. This is also free and very quick. You will need to have a business name - using your username is acceptable. Select the "Basic Advertising Account" for the Pro plan.
